Know about NMREC

Established in 2001, is a leading institution located in Hyderabad, Telangana. Founded by Nalla Malla Reddy, a visionary educationist, the college was built with the mission to foster excellence in technical education and empower students with knowledge, innovation, and values. With its serene, eco-friendly campus and state-of-the-art facilities, NMREC has emerged as a hub for learning, research, and holistic development. The institution is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH), and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). It consistently strives to produce competent engineers, managers, and leaders who contribute meaningfully to society and industry.

Explore Our Courses, Build Your Future

At our institution, we offer a dynamic learning environment across diverse disciplines, equipping students with the skills and expertise to excel in engineering, technology, and business. Whether you aim to innovate in computer science, advance in mechanical design, or lead in business administration, our programs are designed to shape industry-ready professionals.

Computer Science and Engineering

Computer Science and Engineering

Founded in 2001 with 60 seats, CSE expanded to 180 by 2018. With a strong vision, it excels in innovation, research, hands-on training, and industry exposure, ensuring students stay updated with advanced technologies, industry trends, and real-world applications.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The department offers B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering with an intake of 60 students..

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering department began with 60 students and is among NMREC’s most reputed. With expert faculty from IITs, NITs, and industries like ECIL, it offers state-of-the-art labs and student chapters (AME, SAE, ISTE) for holistic professional growth.

Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering, a vital field, has been producing skilled professionals since 2012. The department excels in research, teaching, and industry readiness, blending engineering with management and technology while encouraging extracurricular activities to foster teamwork, leadership, and professional growth.

Master of Business Administration

Master of Business Administration

The department of Management Studies offers two year full time PG Programme Master of Business Administration under choice based credit system. The course is a full time program spanning 4 semesters.
